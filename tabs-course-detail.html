Singapore strives to become a “Smart Nation” through harnessing digital technologies to provide better transport systems, living environment, business productivity, healthcare and public sector services for all Singapore residents. To meet increasing demand the ABC Learning Centre offers the Diploma in Web and Programming. This course covers the essential technical skills in supporting the “Smart Nation” and “Advanced Manufacturing” drives of Singapore. This part-time evening course can be completed in 2.5 years.

This course aims to equip students with good working knowledge and skills required for the deployment, operation and support of computer networks infrastructure, development of info-comm services, computer/embedded systems and IoT systems. The modules have been carefully selected to cover the technical skills and knowledge highly in-demand for career in IT networks and systems industry.

Applicants with any of the following qualifications are invited to apply for the diploma. The following GCE 'O' Level grades and with at least 1 year of relevant working experience:
1. English Language Grade 1 - 7
2. Mathematics (Elementary or Additional) Grade 1 - 63.
3. Science Grade 1 - 6
OR
Nitec with GPA >= 3.5 in an engineering or information technology discipline.
OR
Nitec with GPA >= 3.0 in an engineering or information technology discipline and with at least 1 year of relevant working experience.
OR
Nitec in Technology with GPA >= 3.5 in an engineering or information technology discipline and with at least 1 year of relevant working experience.
